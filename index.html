Executive Summary

The Aura Ecosystem (FurryWatch) is a fully integrated pet safety, health, and behavior monitoring system. It unifies a smart e-collar, intelligent crate, and AI-powered camera into one ecosystem controlled by a single mobile app. Designed for reliability, predictive care, and real-time remote interaction, Aura delivers continuous monitoring through hybrid Wi-Fi/cellular connectivity and a single subscription model. Aura solves the fragmentation and inefficiencies of current pet tech by offering a cohesive, automated, and intelligent pet care platform.

Problems We Solve

  • Fragmented Devices: Current GPS trackers, cameras, feeders, and health monitors each require separate apps and subscriptions.
  • Poor Connectivity: Most devices rely solely on Wi-Fi and fail when the connection drops.
  • Lack of Intelligence: Existing systems rarely include AI for behavioral or health anomaly detection.
  • No Automation: Current solutions cannot take automated environmental action.
  • Limited Remote Control: Owners cannot intervene in real time when away.

Our Solution

The Aura Ecosystem connects three smart devices—an AI camera, GPS/health monitoring e-collar, and intelligent crate— through a unified app with continuous data sharing. The system uses AI for predictive alerts, supports automated crate adjustments, enables real-time interventions, and prevents downtime with hybrid connectivity.

Technical Overview

The system consists of three interconnected hardware components:

  • Smart E-Collar: Tracks vitals (heart rate, temperature), GPS location, stress indicators, and movement patterns.
  • AI Camera Unit: Performs real-time behavior classification and security monitoring.
  • Smart Crate: Automatically adjusts temperature, humidity, and lighting based on real-time pet data.

All devices sync with the Aura mobile app and cloud services through both Wi-Fi and cellular fallback.
