Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Aura Ecosystem work?

The system connects your pet’s smart collar, smart crate, and home app through a secure cloud network. Sensors collect real-time health, GPS, and environmental data, which you can monitor and control through one unified mobile or web app.

What’s the difference between this system and traditional pet trackers?

Unlike standalone GPS trackers or health devices, this ecosystem provides: Combined health + GPS + behavior + environmental management A fully integrated smart crate with automation Predictive AI insights One centralized app for all devices Real-time monitoring even when you’re away

Is the system safe for pets?

Yes. All sensors are non-invasive, and the crate is designed with: Fail-safe gate locking Rounded edges Smart ventilation Overheat protection Wireless communication compliant with pet safety standards

What’s the range of the GPS?

There is no range limit. As long as the collar has LTE connectivity, you can track your pet’s location from anywhere in the world through the app.

How do I get started?

Set up your account in the app > Pair the smart collar > Connect the smart crate to Wi-Fi > Customize alerts, health monitoring, and geofences > Start tracking and controlling your pet’s environment instantly
